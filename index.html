About Me

I was born and raised in Colorado. I earned a bachelor's degree in International Affairs with a minor in Economics from the University of Colorado in Boulder. After working as an English teacher in Seoul, Korea for nearly four years I returned to earn a master's degree at George Mason University. My research interests include the nineteenth-century American West and the National Park Service.
